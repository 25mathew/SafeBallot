Voting Online

Overview

When voting online, there are several steps put in place to ensure that you can safely vote from the comfort of your home.

1 - Registration

When You visit our website, you will first need to register with us. Information required to register for online voting includes your social security number, driver's license, and birth certificate. After registering, you will receive a username; DO NOT FORGET THIS USERNAME. We recommended that you write down or save the username somewhere. It is only issued once, so do not lose it. A password will be mailed to you and will arrive within two days of registration.

Click here to register

2 - Requesting a new password

If it has not arrived after two days, you can request another password using your username and social security number.

Click here to request a new password

3 - Voting

Once you have both the username and password, you can now login to our voting page. Go to the voting login page and enter the username and password. Once you have logged in, you can select the candidates you want to vote for; After you have made your selections, you will submit your ballot. Finally, you will get a confirmation that your vote is received.
